Mexican Black Bean Nachos

Description

A tasty Mexican style black bean nacho mix with corn salsa and sour cream. Quick and easy to make, simple to change and experiment with!

Suitable for beginners in the kitchen and vegetarian (with very simple alterations to make it full vegan!)

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Sweetcorn : 300g>
  • Tomato : 2
  • White Wine Vinegar : 1 TBSP
  • Brown Onion : 2
  • Black Beans : 2 tins
  • Baby Spinach Leaves : 1 medium bag
  • Flour Tortillas : 1 medium Bag
  • Tomato Paste : 60g
  • Spicy Taco Seasoning : 30g
  • Water : 1 cup
  • Vegetable Stock Powder : 10g
  • Butter : 40g
  • Shredded Cheese : 1 large packet
  • Light Sour Cream : 1 regular packet

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220/200°C fan-forced. Drain sweatcorn and finely chop tomato.
  2. In a small bowl, combine corn and tomato. Add a drizzle of white wine vinegar and olive oil. Season to taste and toss.
  3. Roughly chop brown onion and baby spinach. Drain and rinse black beans.
  4. Slice tortillas into wedges and place on lined oven tray.
  5. Drizzle or spray generously with olive oil and season with salt and pepper. Toss to coat.
  6. Bake until lightly golden, 8-10 mins.
  7. Meanwhile, heat a drizzle of olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Cook onion until just softened, 2-3 mins.
  8. Add beans and cook, stirring until softened, 3-4 mins.
  9. Stir in tomato paste and spicy taco seasoning and cook until fragrant, 1 min.
  10. Stir in the water, vegetable stock powder, butter and spinach and simmer until wilted, 1-2 mins. Lightly mash beans.
  11. Divide tortilla chips between plates, top with black bean mixture, cheese and corn salsa. Top with sour cream.
